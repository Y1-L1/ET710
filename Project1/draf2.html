Mountain City

Where is Chongqing?

Chongqing is a vibrant mountain city located in southwest China, famous for its magnificent landscape and unique urban style. There are delicious hot pot, charming night view, Hongyadong built on the mountain, and thrilling light rail passing through the building. The confluence of the Yangtze River and Jialing River here adds more charm to Chongqing. Whether it is historical culture, modern urban style, or natural beauty, Chongqing will bring you an unforgettable travel experience!

Liziba Station

Liziba Station is a unique light rail station in Chongqing, China, famous for its striking design: the metro train runs directly through the middle of a residential building. Located on Line 2 of Chongqing's Rail Transit system, this "train-in-building" phenomenon has become an iconic symbol of the city's innovative urban planning. Visitors can enjoy the spectacle from a nearby viewing platform, making it a must-see attraction for anyone exploring Chongqing's modern and dynamic landscape.

Hot Pot

Chongqing Hot Pot is a fiery and flavorful culinary experience originating from Chongqing, China. Known for its rich, spicy broth infused with chili peppers and Sichuan peppercorns, it offers a unique "mala" (numbing and spicy) sensation. Diners cook a variety of fresh ingredients like meat, vegetables, and noodles in the communal pot at the table. A symbol of Chongqing's vibrant food culture, it's a must-try for anyone visiting the city!
